ACADEMIC TRAINING

Biotechnology is a knowledge-based industry requiring manpower that has the right combination of understanding the technology as well as managerial expertise. The Indian industries in this context face a lacuna for trained manpower, both in terms of specific skill sets and the ability to mature technology into a commercial and fertile industry.

To achieve the above requirements UniBiosys conducts training programmes in Biotechnology and its ancillary fields. The student course will emphasize on practical hands-on training where as the corporate course will aim to give an in-depth overview of the subject.

WORKSTATION FOR PhD/ M.Phil

UniBiosys provides assistance for the candidates who wanted to perform the skilled experiments as a part of their PhD/ MPhil research programmes in Biotechnology, Microbiology, Biochemistry or any life science fields.

FACULTY TRAINING PROGRAMME

UniBiosys provides several training programmes and workshops for professional development of teachers and laboratory technicians. All training programmes are designed to provide hands on experience in most advanced techniques of Life sciences.

CORPORATE TRAINING COURSEWARE

The rapid pace of technological innovation in the bioscience industry has created a growing need for continuous training to scientists and other corporate professionals. To meet this need, UniBiosys developed a courseware specifically for corporate people.

SCIENCE AWARENESS PROGRAMMES

UniBiosys conducts workshops at school and college level to develop a generation of life science students with innovative and creative ideas who becomes an asset to the society. Our activity based learning programme makes studying an enjoyment.
