How to reach UniBiosys Kochi,


By Road : UniBiosys Biotech Research Labs is situated near Cochin University arch, South Kalamassery (Close to CUSAT Metro station). It is about 10 KMS from Aluva and 12 KMS from Ernakulam. If travelling by KSRTC/ City Buses, the nearest Bus stop to the UniBiosys is South Kalamassery (CUSAT).

By Train : If you are coming by train you can alight at Aluva or Ernakulam (Ernakulam Junction OR Ernakulam town).
From there you can reach south Kalamassery by city bus.

By Air : The Cochin International Airport located at Nedumbassery is about 24 kms and a mere 30 minutes drive from UniBiosys.
